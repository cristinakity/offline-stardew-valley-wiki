Festivals are special events in Stardew Valley. Mayor Lewis will send the player a letter in advance of each festival, describing when and where it will take place. On the day of the festival, a pop-up on left-hand side of the screen will let the player know when the festival has begun. If they choose to attend a festival (other than the Éjszakai piac), upon leaving they will be returned to A Farm late at night.

If it is a festival day other than the Éjszakai piac, animals do not need to be fed; the game will automatically consider them fed.

Attendance at festivals is entirely optional. Speaking to villagers at festivals will increase friendship points, the same as any other day. Some festivals can have special effects on friendship, such as increasing friendship with every known villager, or increased friendship for gifting.

In a Többjátékos game, every player who is present needs to enter the festival area to proceed (except for the Éjszakai piac). The same goes for leaving it, if it doesn't end automatically. Generally only the host can initiate further events.

Kereskedők menetrendje

Fő cikk: Kereskedők menetrendje

All buildings and homes in Stardew Valley are "locked" on all festival days where the festival begins before 7pm (i.e., all festivals other than Lelkek Éjszakája és A Holdfény Medúzák Tánca). Csatorna, Közösségi központ, Mozi, és Fürdő will remain open. The exception to this rule is the Éjszakai piac, where homes and shops are open, and time continues to pass during the festival. These buildings remain locked all day, even if the player does not attend the festival.

Tavasz

Tojás Fesztivál

Tojás Fesztivál
Fő cikk: Tojás Fesztivál

A Tojásfesztivál minden Tavasz 13.napján lesz. A játékos elindítja a fesztivált amikor Pelikán Város főterére belép reggel 9 és délután 2 között. A fesztivál befejeződik a játékos visszatér A Farm-ra este 10 órára.

A Tojásfesztivál kiemelt attrakciója a Tojásvadászat. A játékos és néhány másik falubeli kis színes tojások után kutat Pelikánváros főterén. Tojásvadászat azzal kezdődik, hogy polgármesterrel Lewis-al beszélünk. A játékosnak össze kell gyűjtenie 9 színes tojást (50 másodperc alatt, valós időben), különben Abigail nyer. (A többszereplős játékokban 9-nél kevesebb tojás szükséges a nyeréshez.) Ha nyernünk, egy szalmakalapot kapunk jutalmul a nyerés első évében, majd pedig data-sort-value="1000">1 000g aranyat.

Van egy fülke, ahol a játékos dekorációt vásárolhat Kerti Flamingo, Plüss Nyúl, Szezonális Növények valamint dekorációk, és Eper mag. (Ha tavasz 16-án vagy azt megelőzően ülteted el az epermagot, a játékos Nyár előtt kétszer szüretelhet Epreket).

A fesztivál azután ér véget, hogy Lewis polgármester kihirdeti a Tojásvadászat győztesét.

Virágtánc

Virágtánc
Fő cikk: Virágtánc

The Flower Dance event takes place on the 24th of every Tavasz. The player attends the dance by entering Tufanedv-erdő between 9am and 2pm. When the festival ends, the player is returned to A Farm at 10pm.

The dance takes place in the far west of the forest, across the bridge that is south of the Varázsló Torony. This area is accessible only during the Flower Dance.

There is a booth set up where the player can purchase a Dandelion, Daffodil, Tub o' Flowers, Seasonal Decor, Seasonal Plants, and Rarecrow #5.

During this festival, the player can dance with one of the bachelors or bachelorettes, or another player in Többjátékos. They must talk to them (twice) and invite them to dance, but they will refuse unless the player has at least four hearts of Barátság with them. The player can talk to the other villagers and they will have event-related dialogue.

Dancing will increase friendship with their partner by 1 heart (250 points). The dance begins after talking to Lewis. The festival ends after the dance is performed.

Nyár

Luau

Luau
Fő cikk: Luau

The Luau takes place on the 11th of every Nyár. The player attends the Luau by entering Strand between 9am and 2pm. When the Luau ends, the player is returned to A Farm at 10pm.

A central feature of the Luau is the potluck soup. Villagers bring different ingredients to prepare this soup for the Governor, who tastes and judges it. The soup-tasting begins by talking to Mayor Lewis.

Depending upon the type and quality of the ingredients the player supplies for the soup, there will be different reactions from the Governor and Mayor Lewis. The outcome may increase or reduce friendship points with all villagers.

The Luau ends automatically after the Governor tastes the soup.

Holdfény Medúzák Tánca

Holdfény Medúzák Tánca
Fő cikk: A Holdfény Medúzák Tánca

The Dance of the Moonlight Jellies takes place on the 28th of every Nyár. The player enters the festival by entering Strand between 10pm and 12am. When the festival ends, the player is returned to A Farm at 12am.

During the festival, villagers gather at the docks to watch migrating jellyfish who are attracted to the light of a torch that Mayor Lewis lights. Talk to Mayor Lewis to begin the festival.

The festival ends automatically after watching the jellyfish.

Ősz

Stardew Valley Vásár

Stardew Valley Vásár
Fő cikk: Stardew Valley Vásár

The Stardew Valley Fair takes place on the 16th of Ősz every year. The player attends the fair by entering Pelikán Város between 9am and 3pm, and upon leaving the festival, is returned to A Farm at 10pm.

There are several games where the player can gain Star Tokens, a currency that can be exchanged for prizes at the Shop near the entrance to the Buszmegálló.

The Fair also has a Grange Display contest where the player can show the products of the farm and gain Star Tokens. Talking to Mayor Lewis after completing the Grange Display initiates the judging process. Once the items have been judged the player is notified and can return to Mayor Lewis to collect the results. Items displayed in the Grange Display are returned, but not automatically. If a player forgets to collect the items, they can be found in the lost and found box in the Polgármester háza.

During the Fair, the player can eat Survival Burgers cooked by Gus north of Pierre Boltja for free.

The festival ends when the player leaves Pelican Town.

Lelkek Éjszakája

Lelkek Éjszakája
Fő cikk: Lelkek Éjszakája

The Spirit's Eve festival takes place on the 27th of Ősz every year. The player enters the festival by entering Pelikán Város between 10pm and 11:50pm, and upon leaving the festival, is returned to A Farm at 12am.

The festival features a maze where it is possible to obtain the Golden Pumpkin. Pierre has a shopping booth where the player can purchase festive items including a Rarecrow, Jack-O-Lantern, and the Jack-O-Lantern recipe.

The maze is located to the north of Pierre's festive shop. The maze has few dead ends and progresses in a nearly linear fashion. There is no time limit to complete the maze. Towards the end of the maze, in the northwest corner of the map, walk through a block of hedges to the left of a wooden sign with a question mark. Once through, go north through an open cavern and continue east to get to the Golden Pumpkin.

The festival ends when the player leaves Pelican Town.

Tél

Jég Fesztiválja

Jég Fesztiválja
Fő cikk: A Jég Fesztiválja

The Festival of Ice takes place on the 8th of every Tél. The player enters the festival by entering Tufanedv-erdő between 9am and 2pm. When the festival ends, the player is returned to A Farm at 10pm.

At the festival there are ice sculptures and igloos set up as well as a small shop selling festive decorations. There's also an Ice Fishing Contest the player can take part in along with Pam, Willy, and Elliott. The player wins if they catch at least five fish. Players are automatically provided with a fishing rod for the duration of the competition. The first win earns the player two tackles, 1 Magnet bait, and a Sailor's Cap. Subsequent years earn the player data-sort-value="2000">2 000g.

The festival ends after Lewis announces the winner of the Ice Fishing contest.

Éjszakai piac

Éjszakai piac
Fő cikk: Éjszakai piac

The Night Market takes place during Winter 15-17, on Strand, from 5pm-2am. The festival features shops, a mermaid show, and a deep-sea fishing submarine where the player can catch egyedi hal.

Unlike other festivals, shops and homes throughout Stardew Valley are not closed on the days of the Night Market, and time continues to pass during the festival.

Téli Csillag Ünnepe

Téli Csillag Ünnepe
Fő cikk: A Téli Csillag Ünnepe

The Feast of the Winter Star takes place on the 25th of every Tél. The player attends the festival by entering Pelikán Város between 9am and 2pm, and upon leaving the festival, is returned to A Farm at 10pm.

The main event of the Feast of the Winter Star is secret gift-giving. A random villager will be selected to give the player a gift, and they are assigned a random villager to give a gift to. A week beforehand, on the 18th, Mayor Lewis will send a letter telling the player who their gift recipient is. At the festival, talking to their gift recipient will pop up their inventory to decide gift to give. Upon giving a gift, the recipient will give a response and player's gift-receiving cutscene occurs, where they will find out who is giving them a gift at the festival.

The festival ends when the player leaves Pelikán Város.
