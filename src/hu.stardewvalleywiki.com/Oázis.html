The Oasis is a store located in The Desert and run by Sandy, open from 9am to 11:50pm. It sells unique seeds as well as items purchasable from other shops, sometimes at a reduced price.

The path in the back leads to the Casino, run by Mr. Qi, which is accessible after completion of "A rejtélyes Qi" quest.

The Key To The Town does not function at this store.

Notes

  • If Pam is at her doctor's appointment or visiting Ginger Island the bus will not run. This means that you can only travel to the desert by using a Warp Totem: Desert or Desert Obelisk.
  • On Winter 25, despite the Feast of the Winter Star, Pam will still drive the bus as on her regular schedule, allowing the player access to the desert.
  • Emily visits Sandy in the desert on Sandy's birthday (Fall 15), closing the store at 1pm.
