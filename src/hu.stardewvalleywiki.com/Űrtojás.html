The Void Egg is an animal product obtained from a Void Chicken. Initially, a Void Egg can be obtained through a random event involving a witch. The random event can occur as long as the player owns a Big or Deluxe Coop that is not full. The witch will fly over the coop during the night and leave a Void Egg inside.

A Void Egg can also be purchased from Krobus for data-sort-value="5000">5 000g. If the player is married to Shane, there is a chance he may present them with one as a gift on rainy days. A Void Salmon Fish Pond may produce a Void Egg when the population of the pond reaches 9.

A Void Egg sells for a base of data-sort-value="65">65g. This is data-sort-value="15">15g more than a Small Egg, but data-sort-value="30">30g less than a Large Egg.

A Void Egg can hatch a Void Chicken by placing it in a Big or Deluxe Coop's incubator.

A Void Egg will be produced by a Void Chicken daily if fed on the prior day, like all other chickens.

Tailoring

Void Egg is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create a Shirt.  

Quests

  • The Void Egg can be used in the "Goblin probléma" quest to make Void Mayonnaise, which is used to bribe the Henchman in the Witch's Swamp.
  • One Void Egg may be requested by Void Salmon in a Fish Pond quest to increase the capacity of the pond from 5 to 7.

History

  • 1.0: Introduced.
  • 1.1: Removed ability to make into Mayonnaise, added Void Mayonnaise. Now also sold by Krobus. Increased value from 50g to 65g.
  • 1.4: Removed from Traveling Cart stock. Can now be used in Tailoring. Can be requested in a Fish Pond quest. Can be produced by Fish Ponds.
