Below is a list of all shop schedules, including when they are open or closed.

Note: All buildings and homes in Stardew Valley are "locked" on all fesztivál days where the festival begins before 7pm (i.e., all festivals other than Lelkek Éjszakája and A Holdfény Medúzák Tánca). Csatorna, Közösségi központ, Mozi, and Fürdő will remain open. Shops and homes remain locked all day, even if the player does not attend the festival.

The Éjszakai piac festival is unique, in that no homes or shops are locked during festival days (Tél 15-17).

Obtaining the Key To The Town expands access to shops, but (despite its description) it has some restrictions.
