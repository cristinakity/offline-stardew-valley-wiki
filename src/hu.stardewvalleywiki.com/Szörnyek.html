Szörnyek are found in the Mines, the Skull Cavern, the Quarry Mine, the Mutant Bug Lair, and the Vulkán kazamata. When killed, Szörnyetegek have a chance to drop Monster Loot and a variety of items.

Szörnyetegek Farmon

Szörnyetegek can be found at night on The Farm depending on farm type. Szörnyetegek are toggled 'on' by default at the start of the game in the Wilderness Map, or by selecting Advanced Option "Spawn szörnyetegek on the farm" at new game creation. Offering a Strange Bun to the Dark Shrine of Night Terrors in the Witch's Hut will toggle szörnyetegek on/off.

Szörnyetegek on The Farm will scale with the player's Combat level. (At higher levels, more difficult szörnyetegek will spawn, and the Wilderness Golem becomes more difficult to kill).

Veszélyes Szörnyetegek

Szörnyetegek in the Mines and the Skull Cavern become more dangerous during the „Veszély a mélyben” and „Koponya-barlang invázió” quests, or while the Shrine of Challenge is toggled to its 'on' position.

Veszélyes szörnyetegek have unique sprites and stats when compared to their normal counterparts. The Putrid Ghost, Skeleton Mage, and Shadow Sniper also feature unique debuffs.
