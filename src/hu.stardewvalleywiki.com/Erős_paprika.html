Tailoring

Hot Pepper is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine with Cloth in the feed to create a dyeable   Shorts. It is a red dye when used in the spool of the Sewing Machine with a dyeable clothing item in the feed. It can be placed in the red dye pot at Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane, for use in dyeing.

Quests

  • George requests a Hot Pepper by mail on the 25th of Summer in the "Térd terápia" Quest. The reward is data-sort-value="200"> 200g and 1 Friendship heart.
  • Hot Pepper may be randomly requested in Summer at the „Felhívás” board outside Pierre's General Store for a reward of data-sort-value="120"> 120g and 150 Friendship points.

History

  • 1.4: Can now be used in Tailoring.
  • 1.5: Introduced Tropical Curry recipe. Iridium quality is now possible when using Deluxe Fertilizer. Is now an option in the remixed Quality Crops Bundle.
