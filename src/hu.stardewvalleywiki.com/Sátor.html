Sátor
Nyitvatartás: 6-14 között
Cím: Hegyek
Lakók:

Linus

A Sátor Linus otthona, who spends much of his day in and around the tent. It's located on Hegyek northeast of the ácsműhely and east of the passage to the Vasút. It can be entered without first gaining any Barátság points.
