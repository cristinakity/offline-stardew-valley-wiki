The Jade Ring is a ring that can be purchased from the Adventurer's Guild for data-sort-value="2500">2.500g after completing the Deeper in the Mine Quest. It can also be found in Fishing Treasure Chests.

The Jade Ring increases critical strike power by 10% while equipped.
