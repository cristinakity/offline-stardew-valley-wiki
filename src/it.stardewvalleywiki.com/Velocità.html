Speed is a statistic that refers to how fast the player or his/her weapon moves.  Weapon Speed and Player Speed are separate statistics. Each can be affected in a variety of ways.

Food

The following foods increase Player Speed. Coffee is available at The Stardrop Saloon every day for data-sort-value="300">300o, but the other dishes may appear only randomly on Gus' menu.

Note: A single buff of movement speed appears to be a +17% movement speed increase that stacks with the speed increase while riding a Horse, bringing movement speed to a whopping +47%.
