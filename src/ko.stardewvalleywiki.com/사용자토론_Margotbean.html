Hello Idermailer! There are two problems:
(1) New editors must make a series of smaller edits, saving in-between, until they are not considered "new" editors anymore. (You can read 도움말:편집 for more info.)
(2) The Korean wiki doesn't have a "modding" or "모드" section. If there is enough interest, I can request that the server people create a separate namespace called "모드", and then we can start translating it. But so far, there have been only two people who tried to create modding pages here (in the last 6 years). So, I can't add your edits to a namespace that doesn't exist. I'm really sorry, margotbean (토론) 2022년 7월 22일 (금) 15:25 (UTC)

Zombie (토론) 2024년 11월 27일 (수) 01:34 (UTC)

Hello again, Zombie! When you see {{#lst:낚시|fishingrods}} that means the section is transcluded from another page (lst = labeled section transclusion). So, the source page is 낚시 and the section is labeled "fishingrods". If you edit that section on the 낚시 page, it will update automatically on the 도구들 page. This page may provide more helpful info: https://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/Extension:Labeled_Section_Transclusion/ko margotbean (토론) 2024년 11월 27일 (수) 09:48 (UTC)
