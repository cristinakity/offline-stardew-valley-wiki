Pepper Poppers is a cooked dish. It is prepared using either the kitchen inside an upgraded farmhouse or a Cookout Kit.

Pepper Poppers may randomly appear in Krobus' shop on Saturdays or in the Stardrop Saloon's rotating stock. 2 can be gotten by trading a Stardew Valley Almanac at the Bookseller.

Tailoring

Pepper Poppers is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the Tunnelers Jersey. It can be used in dyeing, serving as an orange dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.

Quests

Pepper Poppers are not used in any quests.
