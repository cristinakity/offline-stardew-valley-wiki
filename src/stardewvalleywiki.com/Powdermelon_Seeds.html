Powdermelon Seeds are a type of seed. Mature plants yield Powdermelons.

They cannot be purchased using Gold from any traditional store, and are instead found in various other ways:

  • Breaking crates in the The Mines and the Volcano Dungeon.
  • Digging up Seed Spots between Fall 21 and Winter 20.
  • Trading at the Raccoon Wife (after partway through its questline). It costs 2 Pine Cone.
  • Found in Golden Fishing Treasure Chests between Fall 21 and Winter 20.
  • Using the Seed Maker.
  • Using the Prize Machine in the Mayor's Manor.
  • Drops from barrels in the Skull Cavern between Fall 21 and Winter 20.

Stages

When harvested, each Powdermelon gives 1 Powdermelon. Powdermelon can become a Giant Crop.
