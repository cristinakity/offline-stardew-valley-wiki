Wall below bedroom being unremovable

Since of our wiki says that the Island Farmhouse can be decorated with Furniture, Wallpaper, and Flooring, any wall decoration placed on the wall below the room with two Tropical Beds cannot be removed after placement, this means that no matter how hard we try, it will not be popped off. Is this a bug? Feel free to check other people on YouTube that are using that wall for wall decorations if you can find any. 9000 (talk) 6:45, 2 October 2022 (UTC)

Hello 9000! I am not having a problem decorating the Island Farmhouse, so it seems you have encountered a bug. Bugs must be reported on the official forums, the developers don't monitor the wiki for bug reports. As a side note, Youtube and Twitch are not the best sources of information on the game, because you never know what mods are installed or what version of the game is being used. margotbean (talk) 19:22, 2 October 2022 (UTC)
