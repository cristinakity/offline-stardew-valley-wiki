The Rabbit's Foot is an animal product obtained from a Rabbit with sufficient friendship, mood, and daily luck. It can also occasionally be purchased from the Traveling Cart for data-sort-value="565"1,695–2,825g or dropped by Serpents in the Skull Cavern (0.8% chance).

Despite the description, luck buffs do not influence the chances of getting a rabbit's foot, nor does carrying a rabbit's foot increase luck.

Bundles

Rabbit's Foot is used in the Enchanter's Bundle on the Bulletin Board.

Recipes

Rabbit's Foot is not used in any recipes.

Tailoring

Rabbit's Foot is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create a Shirt. It can be used in dyeing, serving as a yellow dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.

Quests

Rabbit's Foot is not used in any quests.

Other Uses

  • One Rabbit's Foot is used when solving the puzzle depicted in Secret Note #20.
  • Having a Rabbit's Foot in inventory prevents a negative outcome in the Group 10-Heart Event.

History

  • 1.0: Introduced.
  • 1.1: Increased value from 450g to 565g.
  • 1.3.27: Required to solve secret note #20. Can be used in group 10-heart event.
  • 1.4: Can now be used in Tailoring.
