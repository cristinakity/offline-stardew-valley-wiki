Mini-Forge
Now, you can use a dwarvish forge from the convenience of your home.
Information
Source Crafting
Sell Price Cannot be sold
Crafting
Recipe Source Combat Mastery
Ingredients Dragon Tooth (5) Iron Bar (10) Gold Bar (10) Iridium Bar (5)

The Mini-Forge is a portable version of the Forge found on Floor 10 in the Volcano Dungeon. Its recipe is unlocked after obtaining Combat Mastery. It acts exactly the same as the regular forge and can be placed anywhere.

History

  • 1.6: Introduced.
