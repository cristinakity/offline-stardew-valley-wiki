New Renovations

I'm not sure how to fromat the new renovations with their costs and everything. I have prices for some of them if anyone does know: expanded corner room- 100,000; attic- 60,000; cubby-10,000; dining room- 150,000; open bedroom- 10,000

Kalenthi (talk) 21:10, 20 March 2024 (UTC)

Use the template called "price". You surround the word "price" with double curly brackets, { and }. After price, continue with a vertical bar and the number of gold required, without commas. So, inside the double brackets: "price|100000". What you see is: data-sort-value="100000"> 100,000g Giles (talk) 06:22, 21 March 2024 (UTC)

I'm not sure how the renovations would fit with the current table. Should we make a new section/table for them? Kalenthi (talk) 20:12, 21 March 2024 (UTC)
