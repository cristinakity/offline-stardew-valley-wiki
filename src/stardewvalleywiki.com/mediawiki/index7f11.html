Milk is an animal product obtained from a Cow by using a Milk Pail. It can also be obtained from Sandy on Saturdays, who will comment that the player looks thirsty. Additionally, it is sold by Alex during the Desert Festival for data-sort-value="10"> 10 Calico Eggs.

When a cow has a sufficient friendship and mood level, it can produce a Large Milk, which is more valuable.

Tailoring

Milk is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the White Overalls Shirt.  

Quests

  • Milk may be randomly requested during any season at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store for a reward of data-sort-value="375"> 375g and 150 Friendship points. Milk will never be requested unless you own a Cow or Goat. (Owning a goat will result in cow's milk being requested.)

Secret

   Warning: Spoilers   

This page or section contains unmarked spoilers from update 1.6 of Stardew Valley. Players may want to avoid or be cautious toward reading this article/section.

Details  
A milk put on a table or long table would be exchanged for a Mystery Box during the night of Winter 24th, the eve of Feast of the Winter Star.

History

  • 1.0: Introduced.
  • 1.1: Increased value from 100g to 125g
  • 1.3.27: Made all recipes that use milk accept large milk, not just regular milk.
  • 1.4: Can now be used in Tailoring.
  • 1.5: All recipes that use Milk or Large Milk now accept Goat Milk or Large Goat Milk.
  • 1.6: Can now be purchased from Alex at the Desert Festival. Dwarf no longer dislikes milk and is now neutral. Add a secret about Feast of the Winter Star.
