The Galaxy Dagger is a dagger weapon that can be purchased at the Adventurer's Guild for data-sort-value="35000">35,000g after obtaining a Galaxy Sword.

It can be combined with Galaxy Soul (3) and Cinder Shard (60) in the Forge to create an Infinity Dagger.

History

  • 1.1: Introduced.
  • 1.11: Reduced price from data-sort-value="350000">350,000g to data-sort-value="35000">35,000g.
  • 1.5: Can now be changed to an Infinity Dagger. Level decreased from 20 to 8, sell price decreased from data-sort-value="1000">1,000g to data-sort-value="400">400g.
