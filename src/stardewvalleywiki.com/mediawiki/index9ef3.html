Capitalization standard

What is the preference regarding the capitalization of in-game entities? For example, on each of the season pages, references to the surrounding seasons are lowercased, except for in the winter article, in which they are cased as proper nouns. This inconsistency also exists elsewhere, including references to time (e.g., Noon versus noon). Whtqn (talk) 19:44, 2 November 2017 (UTC)

The only rule is that links need to match page names, so if the page name is capitalized, but you want it in lower-case in the body of a page, then use the format [[Pagename|display name]]. Out of laziness, I often leave the link as is, so it's capitalized even when it really shouldn't be. (I also tend to capitalize names of seasons, even though that's not correct.) As long as the link is correct, then it's your choice. With literally hundreds of editors over the past year and a half, there's bound to be a lot of differences. No worries. margotbean (talk) 20:52, 2 November 2017 (UTC)
Think the mediawiki software is case INsensitive on the first letter, so that e.g. Spring and spring should go to the same page. Won't help for Copper Axe and copper axe though (as highlighted by the 'redlink'). Could use heaps of redirects, but not the most fun thing to set up. Presumably capitalisation is not a big problem.
