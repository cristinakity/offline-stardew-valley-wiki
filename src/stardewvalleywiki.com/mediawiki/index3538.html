The Way Of The Wind pt. 1 is a power book that can be purchased from the Bookseller for data-sort-value="15000">15,000g.

The first reading grants the player a power that gives them a permanent +0.25 Speed buff.[1] Once read, it can be found in the Player's Menu on the Special Items & Powers tab. Each subsequent reading gives the player 20 XP in each of the 5 skills.

After reading this book for the first time, the player is able to purchase Way Of The Wind pt. 2 from the Bookseller.

Bundles

Way Of The Wind pt. 1 is not used in any bundles.

Tailoring

Way Of The Wind pt. 1 is not used in any tailoring. It can be used as an iron dye when used in the spool of the Sewing Machine with a dyeable clothing item in the feed.

Quests

Way Of The Wind pt. 1 is not used in any quests.
