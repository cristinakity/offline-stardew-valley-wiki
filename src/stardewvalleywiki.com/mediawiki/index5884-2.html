Calculations

The current calculations are not very insightful as they consider each case individually. It would be more efficient to combine the cases considering we know the chances of each case happening:

On average, every two times the player uses the Heavy Furnace, exactly 11 bars will be produced, using 50 Ore and 6 Coal. With a regular Furnace, we would need to use 55 Ore and 11 Coal to make 11 Bars. Therefore, on average, 45% of the Coal is saved and 9% of the Ore is saved.
