Night Market fish

What counts as "the southwest corner of the Beach" when it comes to fishing for Night Market fish? Is it the southwest pier or does the southwest corner of the actual beach work? I'm inclined to believe it's the pier since that's where the submarine shows up, but even from there, where do you cast your line? Is it west, south, or do you have to angle your cast southwest? I looked at the release notes and that also just says, "southwest corner of the beach". I tried various places and never attracted a Night Market fish, but with all the fish you attract with magic bait that doesn't mean much. It would be nice to have a definitive answer. Zendowolf (talk) 05:44, 14 March 2022 (UTC)

The game code specifies x < 12 and y > 31, which is the top of the base of the submarine and the left edge of the Desert Trader's red rug (and anything left or down from there). I'll upload an image in a bit that highlights the area. margotbean (talk) 15:46, 14 March 2022 (UTC)
Update: I've uploaded the image, but included an accidental space in the file name, so now it will be up to 5 hours before the image is properly visible. After that, it can be added to the wiki wherever you think it's appropriate. Thanks! margotbean (talk) 16:02, 14 March 2022 (UTC)
Everything seems to be working. Thanks! It's more or less where I thought it would be, but it's nice to know for sure. Zendowolf (talk) 18:23, 14 March 2022 (UTC)
