Dialogue for Fall on Thursdays

Since this wiki says that the Abigail quote for Thursdays in Fall says that she dreamt about her hand turning into an object, instead for us, a popup box shows up saying that Abigail is not interested of talking right now. Is it because of Friendship level or other reason? We'll give you some ideas what is causing it. 9000 (talk) 8:43, 20 September 2022 (UTC)

Both dialogues are listed in Abigail's dialogue file for Fall Thursdays. I'm not familiar enough with the file format to answer your question, but you might find the answer on the Modding:Dialogue page. margotbean (talk) 16:33, 20 September 2022 (UTC)

Secret capsule event dialogue

I have a save file in which i am married to Abigail, and on a monday in summer of the sixth year (not sure the date) she had a morning dialogue saying something along the lines of "last night I saw something darker than black running between the trees." I have broken the suspicious capsule in the save file, so it could have something to do with that, anyone else see this on a different spouse? JawaDrawf (talk) 10:03, 12 October 2022

The dialog you saw was exclusive to Abigail, it can be found in Characters/Dialogue/MarriageDialogueAbigail.xnb. It appears that she can say it on any rainy night. margotbean (talk) 18:11, 12 October 2022 (UTC)

Quote for not in a good mood

Since we are familiar with dialogues, Abigail's quote where she says: "I'm not in a good mood right now." then says "I said I'm not in a good mood... what do you want?" Instead for us she says: "Ugh... I'm not in a good right now." "I said I'm not in a good mood... what do you want?" The second dialogue remains the same, but confuses us because the Wiki says "I'm not in a good mood" without the "Ugh..." at the start of the dialogue. We should get people over to the game code to check these out and fix these up... 9000 (talk) 8:46, 12 June 2023 (UTC)

Can you please give more context next time: Is Abby married (to the player or otherwise) and what season/day was it? Since you appear to have the free time to do all these nitpicks, do you want to be the person who goes into the code to clear this up? Atravita (talk) 12:59, 12 June 2023 (UTC)
Here is how the dialogue can be found: Tuesday, Spring, at 0 Hearts. It can be found by other players if you're Married to Abigail if they have 0 Hearts, as long as you're in Spring, and on Tuesdays. 9000 (talk) 8:48, 20 June 2023 (UTC)
