Golden Spur is a trinket item dropped by enemies.

Equipping the golden spur in your trinket slot causes your critical strikes to give you a short speed boost. The spur can be reforged by using an Anvil. The duration of the speed boost ranges from 5 to 10 seconds.

Originally intended to be called Iridium Spur.[1]
