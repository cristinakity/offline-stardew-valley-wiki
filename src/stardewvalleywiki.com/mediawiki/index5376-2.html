Tortilla is a cooked dish. It is prepared using either the kitchen inside an upgraded farmhouse or a Cookout Kit.

Tortilla may randomly appear in Krobus' shop on Saturdays or in the Stardrop Saloon's rotating stock.

Tailoring

Tortilla is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the Tortilla Shirt.   It can be used in dyeing, serving as an orange dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.

Quests

Tortilla is not used in any quests.
