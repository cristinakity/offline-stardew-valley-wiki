What weeds can damage

I see coming up over and over in various places folks being surprised that weeds damaged their x or y item. Someone just recently mentioned on the forum their assumption that weeds can only destroy paths because the wiki article refers only to paths being able to be damaged. I'd go straight to wiki to check, and if I didn't find it there I might understandably think it is an error too if not found here. I'd like to correct this, but I'm not 100% certain what all is included/excluded, the answers online seem to vary. Is it correct to say: Weeds can damage crops, paths, and placable items such as machines, but cannot damage fences, chests or buildings."? Username_Removed (talk) 09:38, 23 June 2021 (UTC)

I tried to search in the code. Here are parts of them in the Gamelocations.cs (Line 12428):
if (object2.name != null && !object2.Name.Contains("Weed") && !object2.Name.Equals("Stone") && !object2.name.Contains("Twig") && object2.name.Length > 0)
{
	flag5 = true;
	Game1.debugOutput = object2.Name + " was destroyed";
}
this.objects.Remove(vector + vector2);
I'm not sure how debugOutput works, but I guess the weeds can destroy all types of objects except for weeds, stone and twig. Horizon98 (talk) 17:16, 23 June 2021 (UTC)

weeds spawning

This page doesn't mention the frequency of weeds spawning in some places, so I did some tests, and here's the result:

  • Railroad: one per year
  • Mutant Bug Lair: everyday
  • ginger island east: everyday
  • The Mines: every time you enter a new level

I'm not 100% sure about it and I don't really know how to check game codes for references, so maybe it's better to start a discussion before edit. >_<

Also, about weeds multiply and spread, the page says especially on day 1 of each season, when it is raining, and during the Summer. Are there any references on this?--Shirotsuki (talk) 19:48, 16 July 2023 (UTC)

Hello Shirotsuki! The reference is already on the page, StardewValley.GameLocation::spawnWeedsAndStones produces more debris when raining and on the 1st of a season. I don't immediately see where Summer comes into the equation, however. That part may (or may not) be outdated.
Some things to keep in mind:
  • Weeds spawn in the Railroad, but the player doesn't have access to it until Summer of year 1
  • Weeds may spawn everyday in the Mutant Bug Lair, but only a few at a time. It's not worth visiting every day, it's better to wait a few days (perhaps several days) for the weeds to build back up, if collecting fiber or other resources is the goal. Same for the jungle on Ginger Island.
  • The Mines only reset when the player exits to the top of the mines (i.e., floor 0, where the Dwarf is).
Hope this helps! margotbean (talk) 00:09, 17 July 2023 (UTC)
Oh, thanks for reminding! I did forget about the earthquake. 😂 And thank you for letting me know where to find the reference! I'd consider how to make more accurate revisions~ --Shirotsuki (talk) 13:52, 19 July 2023 (UTC)
