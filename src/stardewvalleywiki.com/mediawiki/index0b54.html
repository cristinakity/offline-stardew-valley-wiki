It seems that I am unable to trigger the 8-heart event at present, though I do have 8 hearts with him. Could it be because I haven't yet seen his 6-heart event and its follow-on? I know it doesn't work that way with most heart events and other NPCs, but there is something reasonable about this particular possibility in that it reflects the story line much better. Green rain

During green rain, Shane continues a semi-regular schedule and when found in the Saloon says something along the lines of: 'Oh there's a weird weather event? Hadn't noticed'. The page just states that he stays in Marnie's all day, which needs to be edited.

The Green Rain schedule on this page refers to the first time there is green rain, when nearly everyone stays inside the entire day. After the first year, everyone defaults to their usual rainy day schedule.
Since I've only had the chance to test this on new saves, I can't confirm whether the "stay inside all day" schedule only applies to Year 1 or to the first year the player encounters green rain, or how best to handle that distinction if it exists. For that reason, I've put off changing all the villager schedule edits I made to specify that the unique green rain schedule applies to year 1 only.
Of course, it is possible that I just didn't pay enough attention to Shane and missed him going to the Saloon the first year.
Scarecrow (talk) 04:56, 2 April 2024 (UTC)
