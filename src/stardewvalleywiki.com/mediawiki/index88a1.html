Wood Chipper
   
Insert a piece of hardwood to break it down into regular wood.
Information
Source Carpenter's Shop for data-sort-value="1000">1,000g
Sell Price Cannot be sold

The Wood Chipper is used to convert Hardwood and Driftwood into other products. It can be purchased from the Carpenter's Shop for data-sort-value="1000">1,000g starting the first day of Winter in year 1. The player receives a letter about the wood chipper from Robin on the second day of Winter in year 1. A Wood Chipper may be purchased from Kent's shop at the Desert Festival for data-sort-value="25"> 25 Calico Eggs.
