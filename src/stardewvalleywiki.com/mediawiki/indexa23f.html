Minecart

From Stardew Valley Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Destinations available from Pelican Town

The Minecart is a method of travel unlocked after completing the Boiler Room Bundles or by purchasing "Minecarts" from the Joja Community Development Form at JojaMart for data-sort-value="15000">15,000g. They are available for travel the next day.

Minecarts allow the player to travel between four destinations throughout Stardew Valley.
