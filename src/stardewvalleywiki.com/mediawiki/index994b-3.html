The Mushroom Tree Seed is a seed that grows into a Mushroom Tree. It's purchasable from Mr. Qi's shop in Qi's Walnut Room, for data-sort-value="5"> 5 Qi Gems. They can also be obtained from the natural seed spread near a Mushroom Tree, but they do not fall off when Mushroom Trees are shaken.

Tailoring

Mushroom Tree Seed can be used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the Star Helmet It can also serve as a red dye color in the sewing machine or dye pots, located at Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.
