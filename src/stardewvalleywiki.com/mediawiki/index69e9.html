Splitting up the Tailoring Page

I want to put forward the idea that the Tailoring Page should be broken up into separate collection pages for each kind of clothing, due to the size of the page and the number of untailorable items in the game. This way, the Tailoring page can be made smaller, compacting information the tables by removing information not related to tailoring (e.g. descriptions, alternative sources, untailorable items) while still maintaining easy references of all clothing in the game for collectors. This would also facilitate adding extra clothing-related information unrelated to tailoring, for example marking which shirts can/cannot be dropped by the getRandomCosmeticItem() function. Sicarious (talk) 06:15, 10 April 2024 (UTC)
