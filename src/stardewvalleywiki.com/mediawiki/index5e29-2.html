This enemy dropped a galaxy soul for me. Does anyone know how rare this is? Unsigned comment by Thermophile (talk) 11:52, 25 August 2022

For Big Slimes, the chance is 0.9 * 0.005 = 0.0045. That's all I could find in the game code. margotbean (talk) 18:49, 25 August 2022 (UTC)
It's in GameLocation.monsterDrop - after killing 50 hard mode monsters, any hard monster has a 0.1% ( + 0.02*luck %) chance of dropping a galaxy sole. (Slightly less if they're in the secret woods techincally, but hard mode monsters don't appear there outside of mods). Atravita (talk) 18:14, 26 August 2022
Thank you (again) Atravita!! margotbean (talk) 19:27, 27 August 2022 (UTC)
Yep, this did happen to me today, so it does look like any dangerous monster can drop them, just like them dropping Qi Gems. 9000 (talk) 8:43, 30 September 2022 (UTC)
