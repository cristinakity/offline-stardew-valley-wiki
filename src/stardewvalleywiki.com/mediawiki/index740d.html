"Prevents trees"

I was disappointed to see that, contrary to a plain reading of this wiki, trees still randomly sprout and spread on your farm after obtaining the Gold Clock. I'd like to delete this portion of the entry, but will see if anyone has nuance to add that argues for keeping the language in. I find it to be directly contradictory and misleading at worst, and vague at best. Douglitas (talk) 16:38, 20 May 2023 (UTC)

The Gold Clock should prevent new tree seeds from spawning randomly. It doesn't prevent existing fully-grown trees from dropping seeds. If your farm is completely free of trees and has a Gold Clock, and you're finding tree seeds spawning, then you should make a bug report on the official forums. (Use the correct thread for modded or unmodded game, plz!)
I will update the page to reflect that existing trees can still spawn seeds. margotbean (talk) 17:47, 20 May 2023 (UTC)
Is it a fact that seeds spawn without a tree or stump, on the farm? I was under the impression that only mature trees and stumps spawned tree seeds. Douglitas (talk) 00:39, 21 May 2023 (UTC)
Yes, it's a fact. The code is in GameLocation::spawnWeedsAndStones, which is called every day. (Despite the name of the function, it does also spawn trees and twigs.) margotbean (talk) 03:20, 21 May 2023 (UTC)
