Neptune's Glaive is a sword weapon that can be obtained from Fishing Treasure Chests after reaching Fishing level 2.

History

  • 1.5: Level lowered from 7 to 5 and sell price lowered from data-sort-value="350">350g to data-sort-value="250">250g. Bug where weapon defense had no effect fixed.
