Level 14 or 16 in 1.4?

As I was updating weapon changes from 1.4 I had Stardew open in version 1.4.4 and spawned in items via CJBItemspawner to see old 1.4 stats. I noticed that in the 1.4.4 version steel falchion was level 16. Yet the wiki history seems to suggest it was always 14. When was it changed from 14 to 16, or where does the discrepancy come from? Dubesor (talk) 22:25, 30 March 2021 (UTC)

The max damage changed from 38 to 46 with v1.4. Those are the only changes to the Steel Falchion in Weapons.xnb from v1.3.36 to v1.5.4. margotbean (talk) 14:50, 31 March 2021 (UTC)
thanks for the info. I wasn't sure if there were other undocumented changes. I edited the history to reflect the observed changes. Dubesor (talk) 19:23, 31 March 2021 (UTC)
Thank you so much for keeping on top of all these weapons changes! I truly appreciate it!! margotbean (talk) 19:57, 1 April 2021 (UTC)
