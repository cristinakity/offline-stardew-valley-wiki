penny and pam?

in the movies page it says theres a small chance pam will attend a movie when you invite penny, and penny loves any movie if pam is there, but then in bugs it says pam never attends movies randomly when penny is invited?? is there just a chance this can happen to your save file, or is it really impossible for her to appear randomly when penny is invited? thank you in advance!! BaileyBee (talk) 17:38, 29 August 2023 (UTC)

Hello BaileyBee! Unfortunately, it's not possible for Pam to randomly attend if Penny is an invited guest. There is game code indicating that ConcernedApe intended for Penny to love any movie if Pam randomly attends, but then someone (i.e., ConcernedApe) forgot to add a group of people that includes Pam and not Penny to the list of possible random groups of attendees.
So, it can only happen in multiplayer, if one player invites Pam and another invites Penny and then both attend a movie at the same time. Sorry for the bad news. margotbean (talk) 20:29, 29 August 2023 (UTC)

Thank you! thats so sad for pam! BaileyBee (talk) 11:31, 30 August 2023 (UTC)
