Femur
An old, heavy bone caked in centuries of grime.
Information
Type: Club
Level: 2

Stats: Speed (+2)
Adventurer's Guild
Purchase Price: Not Sold
Sell Price: data-sort-value="100">100g

The Femur is a club weapon that is a possible reward for the chest on floor 10 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game. It cannot be obtained in any other way.

History

  • 1.5: Previously unobtainable weapon introduced.[1] Weapon level increased from 1 to 2, sell price from 50 to 100.
