The Iron Dirk is a dagger weapon that can be purchased from the Adventurer's Guild for data-sort-value="500">500g after reaching floor 15 in The Mines. It is also a possible reward for the chest on floor 10 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.

History

  • 1.5: Level lowered from 2 to 1 and sell price lowered from data-sort-value="100">100g to data-sort-value="50">50g. Added as a possible reward on floor 10 in the remixed Mines.
