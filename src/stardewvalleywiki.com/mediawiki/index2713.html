Leather Boots are a footwear item in Stardew Valley. They can be obtained from the chest at floor 10 of The Mines, found in Fishing Treasure Chests after reaching Fishing level 2, or purchased from the Adventurer's Guild for data-sort-value="500">500g after reaching floor 10 of The Mines.

History

  • 1.4: Added the possibility to be purchased in the Adventurer's Guild after reaching floor 10 of the Mines.
