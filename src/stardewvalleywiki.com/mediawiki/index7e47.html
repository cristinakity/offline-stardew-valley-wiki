Abigail

Under Notes - When you talk to Abigail if you are married to her, before the evaluation, she might tell you to keep the foliage around grandpa’s shrine clean, mentioning that it “feels important”.

This happens if marrying Abigail after the 3-year eval, too. Married her Spring 15, Year 3 and she gave the advice the morning of the Flower Dance (Spring 24, Year 3). I had already received eval and claimed reward prior to her mentioning it. Porcupain (talk) 13:31 15 August 2021

It took me some time to find the quote, but it's in her marriage dialogue for any day she's outside. I've updated the page. Thanks! margotbean (talk) 18:29, 15 August 2021 (UTC)

Evaluation dialogue

Evaluation

  • 1 candle
    “ “The farm hasn't changed much in these past few years... but that's okay.

    If you're enjoying your new life, that's all that matters to me...”

  • 2 candles
    “ “You've done a good job with the place.

    It seems you've tried your best... that's all I can ask for. I'm proud of you.”

  • 3 candles
    “ “(male) You've done well, my boy.

    (female) You've done well, my dear.

    <FARM NAME> Farm has never looked better. It's an honor to the family name.

    Grandpa is pleased.”

  • 4 candles
    “ “(male) I'm so proud of you, my boy!

    (female) I'm so proud of you, my dear!

    You're a better farmer than I ever was, and you've brought great honor to the family name.

    I can feel it now... My spirit is finally put to rest. Bless you.”

Reevaluation

  • 1 candle
    “ “The farm hasn't changed much all that much since we last spoke... but that's okay.

    If you're enjoying your new life, that's all that matters to me...

    I must return to the other world, now... but feel free to call on me again when you're ready.”

  • 2 candles
    “ “You've done a good job improving the place since we last spoke.

    It seems you've tried your best... that's all I can ask for. I'm proud of you.

    I must return to the other world, now... but feel free to call on me again when you're ready.”

  • 3 candles
    “ “(male) You've done well, my boy.

    (female) You've done well, my dear.

    <FARM NAME> Farm has never looked better. It's an honor to the family name.

    Grandpa is pleased.

    I must return to the other world, now... but feel free to call on me again when you're ready.”

  • 4 candles
    “ “(male) I'm so proud of you, my boy!

    (female) I'm so proud of you, my dear!

    You've really turned the place around since we last spoke!

    You're a better farmer than I ever was, and you've brought great honor to the family name.

    I can feel it now... My spirit is finally put to rest. Bless you.”

--Spaceeinstein (talk) 18:54, 9 October 2021 (UTC)
