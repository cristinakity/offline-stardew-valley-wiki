Villager paths at The Beach

Since of us playing on the Xbox, when we put objects where the Villagers walk to the location to where they stand and give out scripted dialogue, on Winter 18, if an object was placed on a Villager walking tile, the object gets destroyed as if they were walking through an object anywhere else. This happens because the Night Market map replaces The Beach map on Winter 15, 16, and 17, and it acts like if objects were placed on walking tiles if the Night Market wasn't happening. We were trying to put Sign Of The Vessels next to the Warp Totem location before Winter 15, and Leo and Linus destroyed them. They didn't give out an Angry dialogue bubble over their heads, it just vanished on Winter 18. We think that this might mean that we need an image of the Night Market map of Villager paths being empty slots, with Rotten Plant slots indicating where objects are safe to place without them getting destroyed. 9000 (talk) 9:36, 24 April 2023 (UTC)

Not being able to complete Willy's Bug Meat Quest during the Night Market

On the Special Orders Board, you can pick up Quests from Villagers, and you can collect rewards if you complete them, like a recipe to make Fiber Seeds, a Sewing Machine, a recipe to make Solar Panels, etc. The reward for completing this Quest is the recipe to make Quality Bobbers. But you complete this Quest on The Beach, but when the Night Market is happening, the bin where you put the 100 Bug Meats in next to the Fish Shop is not there during the Night Market, meaning it is impossible to complete this Quest. I've extended the blurb about Willy's Bug Meat Quest line, if that is fine. 9000 (talk) 07:59, 27 January 2024 (UTC)

You are the victim of bad timing. Version 1.6 may or may not change that behavior. margotbean (talk) 20:17, 27 January 2024 (UTC)
