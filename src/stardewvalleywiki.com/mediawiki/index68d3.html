Vinegar is a cooking ingredient that can be used in a variety of recipes. It can be purchased for data-sort-value="200">200g at Pierre's General Store. It can also be sold in George's shop at the Desert Festival for data-sort-value="5"> 5 Calico Eggs. Two Vinegar can also be made by putting Rice into a Keg.

When used on a tree, it prevents the tree from growing Moss.

Tailoring

Vinegar is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the dyeable Orange Gi.  

The Blue sash combined with an orange shirt may be a reference to one of the outfits worn in Dragon Ball Z.
