Highest sell price

The page says it has the highest sell price per single item, but only in Iridium Quality. There are many (not many but some) items with same sell price like Iridium Slime egg and one item with a higher sell price: Tiger Slime Egg with data-sort-value="7500">7,500g. I think this note should be removed or maybe specified on Iridium Quality, or not? Botautal (talk) 20:15, 21 April 2021 (UTC)

It says "potential" sell price -- which means you would have to catch an iridium quality Legend (not likely without mods). I vote to get rid of the note entirely. margotbean (talk) 20:48, 21 April 2021 (UTC)
With a the Angler profession (and maybe the Quality Bobber) the sell price is at least as high as the Tiger Slime egg, but this all in the word potential... I vote to get rid of it, too. Botautal (talk) 21:01, 21 April 2021 (UTC)
