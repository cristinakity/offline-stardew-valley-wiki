Leek surprise gift

Not entirely sure where to put the details for this cutscene after Evelyn's special request, but basically:

Details 

The next time you enter the house there will be wrapped presents all over the lounge/TV room. He will ask if it's some sort of prank, and there will be three choices of dialogue, "We wanted to show our appreciation," "Your wife set this up," and "Yes, it's a prank". Regardless of the choice he opens the biggest present in the middle with red wrapping paper and a green bow, which is a Leek. He will comment it's his favorite like "Mama used to make". While he will harrumph, he acknowledges that he isn't as forgotten as he thought and thank the player (For a female player, "Thanks hun") for making his day. Then the cutscene will end.
