Rewards Are Currently Misleading

I'd like to point out that the page is currently misleading. For example, for the third request, it implies that the Raccoon will accept any type of Dried Mushroom, when in reality, it will ask for a specific type of Dried Mushroom. Therefore, we need to specify the exact type of Dried Mushrooms the Raccoon can request (similarly to how we specify the exact types of Smoked Fishes the Raccoon requests for the first request). We also need to change this for the second and fourth request. Hence, I will re-add the Stub template until this is resolved. User314159 (talk) 21:03, 21 April 2024 (UTC)

Hey! I just filled in the specific mushrooms that can be requested in each season. Before I do all the possible fruits and vegetables, I'd like to clarify how the second and fourth requests are determined. I'm a bit confused, because the way I'm reading Raccoon::addNextIngredient is that each case is equivalent to its own bundle, so case 1 (2nd request) will always ask for fruit products (1 Dried Fruit and 1 Jelly), while case 3 (4th request) will always ask for vegetable products (1 Juice and 1 Pickle). Am I misunderstanding this? Thanks in advance! Bluestblur (talk) 23:33, 21 April 2024 (UTC)
