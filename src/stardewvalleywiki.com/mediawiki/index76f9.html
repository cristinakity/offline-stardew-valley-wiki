Jazz Seeds are a type of seed. Mature plants yield Blue Jazz flowers.

They can be purchased at Pierre's General Store, at JojaMart, from the Magic Shop Boat at the Night Market on Winter 15, and occasionally from the Traveling Cart. They can also be obtained by using the Seed Maker.
