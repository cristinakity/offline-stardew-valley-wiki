Woody's Secret
Felled trees have a 5% chance to yield double the wood.
Information
Source Trees

Woody's Secret is a book that can be found randomly by chopping Trees. The chance for a tree to drop it starts at 0.03% and increases by 0.001% for each tree chopped until it is dropped, at which point the chance becomes 0.1% permanently. However, it cannot drop until the player has chopped at least 20 trees.[1]

It grants the player a power that gives felled Trees a 5% chance to yield double wood. After reading for the first time, additional copies can be read to gain 100 Foraging XP each. It can be traded to the Bookseller in exchange for Hardwood (20).

Once read, it can be found in the player's menu on the Special Items & Powers tab.
