Details on reaction and friendship 

The game runs through these checks with the precedence in this order:


Mayor shorts result:

Item added is mayor's shorts


Best results:

Item of gold or iridium quality with a base price of 160 or greater

or

Item of silver quality with a base price of 300 or greater and an edibility greater than 10

Friendship with all eligible villagers will be increased by 120 for that player


Good result:

Item edibility is greater or equal to 20

or

Item base price is greater or equal to 100

or

Item base price is greater or equal to 70 and quality is silver or greater

Friendship with all eligible villagers will be increased by 120 for that player


Neutral result:

Item base price is greater than 20 and edibility is greater or equal to 10

or

Item base price is greater or equal to 40 and edibility is greater or equal to 5

No friendship change for the player who added this item


Bad result:

Item edibility is greater or equal to 0

Friendship with all eligible villagers will be decreased by 50 for that player


Worst result:

Item edibility is greater than -300 and less than 0

Friendship with all eligible villagers will be decreased by 100 for that player


With multiple players Governor's reaction will be the mayor's shorts response if they are added, the missing item response if less items were added than the current number of players, or the worst of the added item responses. In a multiplayer game, the Governor's response does not dictate the friendship gain or losses for everyone, but instead the item added by each player will only affect that player's friendships with the villagers.

(note: if mayor's shorts are read, the game does not evaluate any other items that have been put in the soup. This may mean that a different player's friendship level may or may not be affected by the item they themselves put in, depending if the non-shorts player's item is evaluated before or after the shorts are)
