The Pirate's Sword is a sword weapon that can be purchased from the Adventurer's Guild for data-sort-value="850">850g after reaching floor 25 in The Mines. It is a possible reward for the chest on floor 50 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.

History

  • 1.5: Level lowered from 4 to 2 and sell price lowered from data-sort-value="200"> 200g to data-sort-value="100"> 100g. Added as a possible reward on floor 50 in the remixed Mines.
