Wedding outfits now added on the day of the ceremony?

My character's outfit was changed to this on his wedding day. It (that is, the shirt and pants) was not what he went to bed in. I'm uncertain if this image is helpful, but I felt I should include it. Cheetospotato (talk) 14:28, 15 November 2021

You bring up an interesting point: the only images we have of wedding ceremonies are from v1.2 and before (mostly before), and the female players are wearing their chosen outfits for the ceremony. (There is only one image of a male player, and he's wearing the suit top and black pants, which doesn't tell us anything.)
My advice is to report this on the official forums, either as a bug or a suggestion (a bug report will probably get more visibility), so we can see if this is intended behavior.
You can include links to these images, if you like:
  • https://stardewvalleywiki.com/File:Marriage_abigail.png
  • https://stardewvalleywiki.com/File:Sebastian_marriage_ceremony.jpg
  • https://stardewvalleywiki.com/File:Harvey_Marriage_Ceremony_2.jpg
  • https://stardewvalleywiki.com/File:Elliot_Wedding.jpg
Since the image you uploaded isn't particularly useful (given that we know female characters wear a white dress and male characters wear a suit top & black pants), I'm going to delete it.
Best of luck in getting to the bottom of this potentially undesirable behavior! Thank you, margotbean (talk) 20:26, 15 November 2021 (UTC)
Playing v1.5.6 (Steam), this no longer happens; the player's character wears their chosen outfit when marrying bachelors & bachelorettes. Going to remove it from the page, and the best I can figure, the fix happened in v1.5.6, although it's not specifically in the change notes. margotbean (talk) 15:54, 23 April 2022 (UTC)
