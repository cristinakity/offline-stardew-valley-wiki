Summer Schedule

Penny's Sunday schedule for Summer indicates that she goes to the Carpenter's Shop and spends the bulk of the afternoon there, and makes no mention of her sitting on the bench by the Community Center. This appears to be an error; on Summer 28 (a Sunday), I encountered her sitting on the bench by the Community Center, and from what I can tell, she never went to the Carpenter's Shop that day. This is also not covered by any of the options listed in her Deviations. Sk00gle (talk) 15:31, 1 August 2021 (UTC)

The schedule data files are notoriously complex. There is an entry for "summer" that has her in town most of the day, then later in the data file there is an entry for "Summer_Sun" meaning Sundays in Summer, where she goes to the Carpenter's shop from noon to 6pm. I don't know which one takes precedence under what conditions. It's an ongoing problem for all villagers' schedules, unfortunately. margotbean (talk) 17:42, 1 August 2021 (UTC)

Penny's friends

Wouldn't Maru also be one of Penny's friends? They are often found sitting together on a bench. Are there specific criteria for what constitutes a friend on the Wiki? Bigfarma (talk) 00:18, 5 November 2022 (UTC)

Maru's page lists Penny as a friend, btw. Any reason not to add Maru to Penny's friends? Bigfarma (talk) 00:23, 5 November 2022 (UTC)

Yep, it's explained on Template:Infobox villager, the "friends" field should be limited to people who will give gifting hints about the villager. It also includes romantic interests, both of which are listed in the data file NPCDispositions.xnb.
If it helps, it's a source of confusion (and sometimes heated debate) on the wiki, and I'm working out a way to make the purpose of the field more clear. Perhaps I should eliminate it altogether, and leave friendships to the "Relationships" section of the page. In any case, I'll remove her from Maru's page for consistency in the meantime. Thanks! margotbean (talk) 04:40, 5 November 2022 (UTC)

Thank you for the explanation! Bigfarma (talk) 13:28, 5 November 2022 (UTC)
