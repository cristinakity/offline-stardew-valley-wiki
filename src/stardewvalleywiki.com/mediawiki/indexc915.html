Mod compatibility page

Hi! The mod compatibility page only tracks SMAPI mods, meaning C# mods which use SMAPI's modding API. Your mod Dragon's Advancing Sprinklers is a content pack for Content Patcher instead; content packs generally depend on the compatibility of the SMAPI mod which loads them, and can't be mass-tested the same way as SMAPI mods. —Pathoschild (talk) 19:40, 8 March 2021 (UTC)

Alright, I'll remove it. I feel like the section in the wiki should clarify that or the sub-heading should say something like "excluding working content patcher mods".--Dragon3025 (talk) 21:14, 8 March 2021 (UTC)
