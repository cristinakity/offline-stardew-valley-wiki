Attack is a statistic that affects how much damage the player does. It can be affected in a variety of ways.

Buff

The following buffs affect Attack.

Warrior Energy +10 Attack Killing an enemy while wearing a Warrior Ring. 5 Seconds
Weakness -20 Attack Getting hit by a Blue Squid's orb 10 Seconds
