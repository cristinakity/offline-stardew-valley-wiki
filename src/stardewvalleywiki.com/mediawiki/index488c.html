Woodcutter's Weekly
Read this to gain some foraging experience.
Information
Source Bookseller • Fishing Treasure Chests • Trees • Desert Festival
Sell Price data-sort-value="500">500g

Woodcutter's Weekly is a skill book that can be obtained by purchasing from the Bookseller or from the Traveling Cart or obtained randomly from Fishing Treasure Chests or by cutting down trees. It is also sold by the Calico Egg Merchant at the Desert Festival for data-sort-value="100"> 100.

It can also be found randomly from Crates and Barrels in the Mines, level 33.

Upon reading the book, players will earn 250 Foraging XP. It can be traded to the Bookseller for 100 Wood.
