Golden Walnuts & Friendship

About how to start friendship with Leo, the page says: "This is done by giving 10 Golden Walnuts to the first parrot outside by the turtle."

Just tested it in-game, giving golden walnuts to parrots is not necessary. To make friends with Leo, players simply need to collect a total of 10 golden walnuts, then enter Leo's hut to trigger the cutscene in which Leo tells the player his name. If you use cheats, this can be triggered even before you give the parrots any walnuts.--Shirotsuki (talk) 13:40, 21 April 2023 (UTC)

Before we change the page, do you have to give the parrot inside Leo's hut 1 golden walnut before the cutscene triggers? I don't have a test save for this scenario... Thanks! margotbean (talk) 16:16, 21 April 2023 (UTC)
The cutscene can be triggered without giving that 1 golden walnut. I used cheats to test how it's triggered, so I just created a new save, teleported to ginger island, added 10 golden walnuts in my wallet, and walked into Leo's hut, then the cutscene is immediately triggered. However, in a normal gameplay without cheats, players can't collect a total of 10 walnuts without giving that 1 golden walnut.--Shirotsuki (talk) 18:14, 21 April 2023 (UTC)
I've updated the page, thank you! margotbean (talk) 17:56, 22 April 2023 (UTC)
