Frog Egg
Summons a hungry frog companion.
Information
Source: Slaying enemies
Adventurer's Guild

Purchase Price: N/A
Sell Price: data-sort-value="1000">1,000g

The Frog Egg is a trinket item dropped by enemies.

Equipping the Frog Egg in the trinket slot spawns a frog which follows you around. When enemies are present (around 4 tiles away from the frog), it consumes the nearest enemy by grabbing it with its tongue. The frog will then follow the player with a full belly. After returning to normal size, the frog will eat the next enemy. It takes 12 seconds after eating until the frog is able to eat another enemy.

Note that enemies killed by the frog will not drop anything, and do not count as kills towards quests or Monster Eradication Goals.

Trinket frog colors, from left to right, top to bottom: brown, green, blue-green, red, yellow, blue, void, and various of the colors that prismatic cycles through

The egg can be reforged using an Anvil to change the color of the frog. There are 8 possible colors of frog. The color of the frog is cosmetic, and does not affect its functionality.

Frog Type Probability[1]

Brown 21.3%
Blue-Green 21.3%
Blue 4.3%
Red 4.3%
Yellow 4.3%
Void 1.6%
Prismatic 1.6%

The frog egg can also be put inside a Fish Tank, hatching a frog of that colour.
