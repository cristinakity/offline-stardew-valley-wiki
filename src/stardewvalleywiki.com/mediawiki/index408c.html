Kent's Rank

The Trivia says Kent's Chevrons are upside down. But they're not. In the UK, the Chevrons are the right way up for Corporal. In China, the Chevrons are the right way up for Private 1st Class. In Russia, two stripes makes a Corporal. The game doesn't take place in the UK, of course. Or in China. Or Russia. But it doesn't take place in the USA either. The US isn't the only country with an army. Come on, this is a world-wide wiki. Breech Loader (talk) 09:37, 11 July 2021
