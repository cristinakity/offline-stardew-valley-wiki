Jewels Of The Sea
Fishing treasure chests have a chance to yield roe.
Information
Source Fishing Treasure Chests • Bookseller

Jewels Of The Sea is a power book that can be obtained from Fishing Treasure Chests. The chance for a treasure chest to contain the book starts at 2% and increases by 0.1% for each treasure chest opened until it is obtained. Then the chance stays at 2.1% permanently.[1]

It can also be purchased from the Bookseller for data-sort-value="20000">20,000g starting in Year 3.

The first reading grants the player a power that makes all fishing treasure chests have a 25% chance to yield Roe.[1] The type of roe obtained is the same as the fish that was caught. Once read, it can be found in the Player's menu on the Special Items & Powers tab. Subsequent readings give the player 100 Fishing XP.

The Bookseller trades either 3 Cave Jelly, 3 River Jelly, or 3 Sea Jelly for 1 Jewels Of The Sea each provided the player has already obtained the power from this book.
