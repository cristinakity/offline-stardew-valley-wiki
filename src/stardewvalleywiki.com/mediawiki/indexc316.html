The Fairy Rose is a flower that can be grown from Fairy Seeds in 12 days.

If grown within proper range of a Bee House (5 squares in any cardinal direction), a Fairy Rose will cause Fairy Rose Honey to be produced, increasing the honey base value from data-sort-value="100">100g to data-sort-value="680">680g. This is the most valuable type of honey, compensating for the poor profit margins on the flower itself.

Fairy Roses can be purchased from the Traveling Cart for data-sort-value="290"870–1,450g.

Bundles

Fairy Rose is one of the options for the Garden Bundle in the Pantry (Remixed)

Tailoring

Fairy Rose can be used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the dyeable Long Dress. The fairy rose can also be used as a red dye pot color for dyeing, located in 2 Willow Lane, Emily and Haley's house.

Quests

Fairy Rose is not used in any quests.

Notes

  • Fairy Roses have an equal chance of being one of six different colors. Fairy Roses of different colors will occupy different slots in inventory or chests. Six colors × four quality levels = 24 possible spaces used.
  • Despite not having a true red variation, Fairy Roses are used as a red dye in the dye pots.
  • Having at least one mature Fairy Roses on the farm by the end of a day increase the chance for the Crop Fairy event by 70% (from 1% to 1.7%).[1]

History

  • 1.4: Can now be used in Tailoring.
  • 1.5: Fairy Dust recipe introduced. Iridium quality is now possible when using Deluxe Fertilizer. Bugfix for crop stages with Deluxe Speed-Gro + Agriculturist.
  • 1.6: Now affects the chance for the Crop Fairy event.
