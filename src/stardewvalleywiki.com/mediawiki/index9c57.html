6+ Hearts Quotes in different Seasons

Since Emily's dialogues are familiar enough, we've found out that different dialogues such as if you're at 6+ Hearts with Emily, she can say the following:

"Oh, hi... It's nice of you to visit me like this." "Have faith... every day you're taking one more step on the path of your destiny." "Do you ever think about the texture of fabric, Player? Each texture has a distinct personality... feel my dress... see?"

Which is odd to us because these Dialogues can be found regularly, these Dialogues are only available in Spring, which is very odd... As an example, In Spring on Saturdays at 6+ Hearts, Emily says the following:

"Do you ever think about the texture of fabric, Player? Each texture has a distinct personality... feel my dress... see?"

Whereas in Summer on Saturdays, Emily says the following:

"Don't overwork yourself or you might end up in Harvey's clinic!"

This is something we should get lads to the game code to check out these dialogues. 9000 (talk) 9:01, 20 June 2023 (UTC)

New custom dialogue for gifting gems while married in 1.6

So CA added various custom dialogue for gifting the different gemstones to Emily in 1.6 and while those have been added to the wiki, I'm noticing on my save file that is married to Emily, I'm getting vastly different dialogue that may be different due to my marriage.

For example, Wiki says the emerald dialogue is "Oh!... Heheh... I know exactly why you gave me this stone today. Very interesting..." but Emily on my end says "Wow, it's beautiful! This colour makes me think of all the spiritual power within the forest... Can you feel that wood energy?"
