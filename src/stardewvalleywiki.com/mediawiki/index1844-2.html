Barn doors

Hi there. This page says "Barn Animals may be stuck outside at night if the barn doors are closed." I've actually observed consistently now that closing the barn doors does not keep my animals from entering the barn when it's time for them to go in. I don't want to make a big change like that, and I'm also not sure what other pages make the same note. Anybody want to confirm this? Tlsafir (talk) 08:53, 29 March 2022 (UTC)

Have you read the section on the Animals page that the Barn page refers to: Animals#Animals Trapped Outside? Have you tried going to bed before 5pm? It was always difficult to trigger the event, but not impossible. margotbean (talk) 17:01, 29 March 2022 (UTC)
