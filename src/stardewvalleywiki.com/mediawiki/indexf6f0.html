The hat shop in fall.

Hats are cosmetic clothing items that alter the appearance of the player but otherwise have no effect.

Hats cannot be sold through the Shipping Bin, to the Hat Mouse, or to any store/shop in the game.

Obtaining hats

The day after earning their first Achievement, players will receive a letter from the mouse in the Abandoned House alerting them that hats can now be purchased there. Most Achievements unlock a new hat for sale.

A few hats can only be obtained at certain festivals, the Casino, the Adventurer's Guild, or in treasure rooms in the Skull Cavern.

Many hats can also be tailored at the sewing machine in Emily's house or the Sewing Machine received as a reward for the "Rock Rejuvenation" special order.

There is a small chance to receive certain hats from slaying monsters, breaking crates and barrels, chopping trees, shaking trees with seeds, digging Artifact Spots, or panning. Details are found on their respective pages.[1]

Secrets

  • Hats can be placed on unexpected things.
  • Pans can be placed into the player's hat slot to save inventory space.

Notes

  1. ↑ See Utility::getRandomCosmeticItem and Utility::trySpawnRareObject in the game code. Chances are determined in GameLocation::monsterDrop, BreakableContainer::releaseContents, Tree::performToolAction, Tree::shake, GameLocation::digUpArtifactSpot, and Pan::getPanItems.
  2. ↑ WeGame is a gaming platform that only operates in mainland China. It is created by Tencent.

History

  • 1.3.27: Added 3 new hats: Blue Cowboy Hat, Red Cowboy Hat, and Cone Hat. Hats can now be placed on horses.
  • 1.4: Added 35 new hats. Hats can now be placed on children, and removed from horses and children.
  • 1.5: Added 19 new hats. Hats can now be placed on sea urchins in fish tanks.
  • 1.6: Added 28 new hats. Hats can now be placed on cats and dogs. Certain hats may now be found via various player actions, similar to the Living Hat. Increased the price of many hats sold by the mouse in the Abandoned House.
