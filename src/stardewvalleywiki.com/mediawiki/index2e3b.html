This is a work-in-progress guided for Skull Cavern! This guide is meant to be read by beginners who are new to this kind of content.

The Basics

Skull Cavern is different from the run-of-the-mill Mines. Your main focus is not to slay as much monsters as possible, it is to go down as far as possible. This is because the further you go down, the more   Iridium Nodes spawn. The best selling point of Skull Cavern is the amount of Iridium Nodes present, and the main goal is to acquire as much   Iridium Ore as possible. Skull Cavern is a race against time, fortunately for you, time is slowed down here (unless you're in co-op.) Whereas one hour of game time normally lasts 43 seconds, one hour here lasts 54 seconds.

Strategies

Tip: The monsters are incredibly dangerous, so it's important to keep your   Health and   Energy as high as possible. Good food sources for this can be found in the What to Prepare section.

  • Do not go out of your way to pick fights with monsters. You will only waste time.
  • Do not bother finding a Staircase on spiral floors, infested floors, prehistoric floors, and other floors that usually take a longer amount of time to exit than normal floors. Use a Staircase and move on.
  • Always keep a pair of Staircases when you're in a sticky situation, especially for the strategy above. A lot of Serpents are chasing you? Staircase down and move on.
  • Once you find a shaft, go down. Shafts are the priority. Dropping down will never put you below 1 Health, but make sure you always check your Health when you fall.
  • Items will still collect in your inventory even if you're pausing the game. Take advantage of this.
