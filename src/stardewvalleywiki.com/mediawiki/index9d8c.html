Android edit app?

Whats the best way to edit on android saves?Jaws (talk) 00:05, 25 August 2021 (UTC)

Hello Jaws! I'm not sure I can best answer your question, but I believe you have to transfer them to a computer first, edit them, then transfer them back to your phone. Make sure to back up the files before editing!
You might find more knowledgable folks to help answer your question on the forums or discord server (links in the left sidebar). They may be able to guide you through specific edits. Sorry I can't be of more assistance! Good luck! margotbean (talk) 17:24, 25 August 2021 (UTC)
Yep, that's pretty much it. The save format on Android is the same as on PC, so Saves#Edit a save should work for Android saves too. —Pathoschild (talk) 17:54, 25 August 2021 (UTC)
