The Duck Egg is an animal product obtained from a Duck. It may be placed in a Big or Deluxe Coop's incubator to create a Duck. Duck Eggs may also be available at the Traveling Cart for data-sort-value="95"285–1,000g.

Tailoring

Duck Egg is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the dyeable Green Jacket Shirt.

Quests

Duck Eggs are not used in any quests.
