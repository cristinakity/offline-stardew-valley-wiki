Does not work in movie theater - what about Joja mart?

despite the description claiming "Allows access to ALL buildings in town" it does not work on the movie theater, attempting to enter it outside of the official opening hours just behaves as if you have no key. I would want to test this on Joja mart too but I don't have a Joja save file anymore. Dubesor (talk) 15:30, 18 March 2021 (UTC)

I have a save file with Joja Mart Membership and the Key to the Town: It works at the Joja Mart (I went in there at 6:50 instead of 8) but it also doesn't work at the Movie Theatre, built from Morris. Botautal (talk) 10:52, 5 April 2021 (UTC)
Oke, thanks for checking. Dubesor (talk) 12:44, 5 April 2021 (UTC)

Pierre's General Store

I was running a test save for gifting purposes, but I entered Pierre's General Store at about 7am, and witnessed Pierre walking from behind the sales counter to the aisle where he stands after 5pm. He didn't return to the sales counter until 8:30am.

Anyone with similar or differing experiences, please comment! The schedule files are no help in this situation. Thanks, margotbean (talk) 18:27, 3 April 2021 (UTC)

I tested a few days: Spring 6-9 (Sat-Mon), Spring 12 (Fr/sunny), Spring 20 (Sat/rain), Fall 28 (Sun/sunny) and Summer 22 (Mon/sunny): On Spring 6/Sat(sunny) he stayed behind counter from 6:20 til 9:00 (when I stopped). And all the other days I checked he stands behind counter 6:20 (probably 6 but couldn't get there faster) until 7:00. He then moves to the shelf until 8:30 and then back behind counter. This seems in line with his schedule file: 700 SeedShop 10 20 0/830 SeedShop 4 17 2. I didn't notice this at first when adding the alternative Shop Schedules, so I will change that. Dubesor (talk) 13:39, 4 April 2021 (UTC)
Thanks so much! I think this has to be a bug, it couldn't be what ConcernedApe intended... margotbean (talk) 16:01, 4 April 2021 (UTC)

Doesn't work on Elliot's beach shack

Instead of considering Eliot's Shack as a home it instead considers it to be his room so the key to the town doesn't work, instead you need to elevate your friendship with him to at least 2 hearts, then you can enter.
