The Chicken Mask is a hat that can be purchased from the Abandoned House for data-sort-value="2000">2,000g after earning the "A Big Help" Achievement (complete 40 'Help Wanted' requests).

Trivia

  • This hat could be a reference to Space Quest III: The Pirates of Pestulon. In it, the player can purchase a hat called the Official Astro Chicken Flight Hat, and is told "you'll really turn some heads in this sporty little number!"

History

  • 1.0: Introduced.
  • 1.4: Fixed typo "thim" to "them" in description.
  • 1.6: Purchase price increased from data-sort-value="1000">1,000g to data-sort-value="2000">2,000g.
