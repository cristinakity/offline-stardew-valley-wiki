Possible Missing Quote

Playing as a male character and being married to Sebastian, I agreed to adopt a second child and Sebastian said, “Ducky, I filed our adoption papers. Now all we can do is cross our fingers and wait.” First of all, my character is not named Ducky, he has my own name, although perhaps this falls under “terms of endearment”(?), second of all, none of the quotes involving adoption are included in the first place, only the ones having to do with pregnancy.
