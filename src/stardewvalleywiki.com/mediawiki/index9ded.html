Health Gain

The article's infobox lists a healing effect of +30, with an energy increase of +45. That makes the H/E proportion 67%, whereas with every other item that proportion is 45%. I calculate the standard proportion to rate a healing effect of +20. Therefore, I suspect a typo in the box, and have made the change in the article. Giles (talk) 04:50, 9 February 2022 (UTC)

Actually, if you look in the game code at the healthRecoveredOnConsumption() method for the Object class, you find that bug steak has a hard coded H/E proportion that is different than all other consumables. I am changing the healing effect back to 30. Squigs44 (talk) 06:03, 9 February 2022 (UTC)
Thanks for checking up on that. I don't have the game code. Giles (talk) 22:02, 9 February 2022 (UTC)
