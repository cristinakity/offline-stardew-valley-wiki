The Iridium Scythe is a tool obtained from the Mastery Cave after choosing the Farming Mastery path. It can scythe grass (yielding hay) and break fiber, similar to the Scythe and Golden Scythe. It can also harvest items grown under tilled soil within its swing range. This includes crops, Spring Onions, and forageables grown from seasonal wild seeds on the farm.

It is the most powerful version of the Scythe. It increases the effectiveness of the Scythe and Golden Scythe by a substantial amount and adds a purple hue.
