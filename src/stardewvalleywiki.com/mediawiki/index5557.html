Sell Price

I don't know what's going on, but, for me, the sell price for the Wedding Ring is data-sort-value="2000">2,000g, and I can sell it only in the Shipping Bin, but not in the Adventurer's Guild as Dubesor said. To do my test I used the mod CJB Item Spawner to get Wedding Rings. Does this mod change anything? Even opening the game without mods, the sell price is still data-sort-value="2000">2,000g. JaksStrange (talk) 18:26, 20 April 2021 (UTC)

Yes, the ring that is spawned with CJB Cheats does not behave the same way as one crafted in a multiplayer game. I have tested in multiplayer, and the ring can be sold to the Adventurer's Guild, for data-sort-value="1000">1,000g (half the price listed in ObjectInformation.xnb). margotbean (talk) 18:34, 20 April 2021 (UTC)
I see, thanks for clarifying this. I apologize for my mistake. JaksStrange (talk) 18:38, 20 April 2021 (UTC)
FYI: Item spawner is handy but fairly unreliable method of testing real item properties. Many things change when spawned in, in contrast to the "real" scenario. For example, of the top of my head, the dark sword will not have the vampiric enchant, or special items such as Broken Trident & Neptune's Glaive don't have their "protection" attribute when only spawned in (e.g. cannot be dropped/trashed). There are many more differences between spawned -vs- legit items but those came to my mind. Dubesor (talk) 18:57, 20 April 2021 (UTC)
