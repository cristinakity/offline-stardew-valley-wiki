Oasis
Open Hours: 9:00am to 11:50pm
Closed: Most festival days, Fall 15 after 1pm
Address: The Desert
Occupants:

Sandy

The Oasis is a store located in The Desert and run by Sandy, open from 9am to 11:50pm. It sells unique seeds as well as items purchasable from other shops, sometimes at a reduced price.

The path in the back leads to the Casino, run by Mr. Qi, which is accessible after completion of "The Mysterious Qi" quest.

The Key To The Town does not function at this store.

Notes

  • On Winter 25, despite the Feast of the Winter Star, Pam will still drive the bus as on her regular schedule, allowing the player access to the desert.
  • Emily visits Sandy in the desert on Sandy's birthday (Fall 15), closing the store at 1pm.

History

  • 1.3.27: Added Cactus Seeds and Seasonal Plant to fixed stock.
  • 1.4: Added random shirt to fixed stock. Limited to 3 Omni Geodes on Wednesday.
  • 1.5: Added Wall Cactus to stock. Coconuts are limited to 10. Sandy leaves the Oasis on Fall 15 (her birthday) at 1pm to visit with Emily. Added a bench next to the Oasis where the player can sit.
  • 1.6: Added mannequins, coat stand, and clothesline to shop inventory. If Pam is not available the player can drive the bus
