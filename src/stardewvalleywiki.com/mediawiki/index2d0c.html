Rather than maintain two nearly identical sets of bundle tables, I was thinking about how we might have one set of tables that serves for both Normal Bundles and Remixed Bundles. New players will only know a world in which both options exist, so it seems like an integrated view would be best. Anyway, I mocked up an idea I had at User:Zendowolf/Sandbox/Bundles. It only shows a few tables--just to give you an idea of what they might look like. Take a look if you're so inclined. Zendowolf (talk) 10:19, 21 March 2021 (UTC)

I think that could work if there was an icon for both "normal" and "remixed" bundles, to keep things clear. On the other hand, I don't imagine the bundles require that many changes, so I don't know if maintaining two copies is a big deal. - Odin (talk) 13:33, 21 March 2021 (UTC)
It's not a bad idea, but there are a few issues. The pages have already been together and then separated. I don't think we need to integrate them again. Also, Template:Bundle needs to be updated every time the headings/sections change on either page. Thirdly, as Odin said, we don't have an official icon for remixed bundles. Any other icon we use could cause confusion. margotbean (talk) 14:19, 21 March 2021 (UTC)

Changes Required

I recently discovered my original unsterstanding of how these random bundles are built is wrong. I will be revamping this page in the near future. Some of the "random" bundles have an index field, which means they can only go in a certain slot. I.e. Rare crops and Quality crops share an index; You will always get one of these two bundles, and never both. BlaDe (talk) 20:44, 24 September 2021 (UTC)

Sounds good, thank you! 👌👍 margotbean (talk) 21:51, 24 September 2021 (UTC)
