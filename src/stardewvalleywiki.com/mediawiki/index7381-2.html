Smoked Fish is an Artisan Good made from the Fish Smoker using any fish and 1 coal, taking 50 minutes. It doubles the sell price of the fish while retaining quality, as well as multiplying the energy and health restoration by 1.5.

Smoked fish benefit from the Fisher and Angler professions, as well as the Artisan profession.

With the Artisan profession, the smoked fish can be sold for 2.8 times as much as the original fish. Sell price = base fish price (x2 smoking bonus) (x1.4 Artisan profession bonus) = x2.8 of the base fish price

With the Angler profession, the smoked fish can be sold for 3 times as much as the original fish. Sell price = base fish price (x2 smoking bonus) (x1.5 Angler profession bonus) = x3 of the base fish price

With the Artisan and Angler profession, the smoked fish can be sold for 4.2 times as much as the original fish. Sell price = base fish price (x2 smoking bonus) (x1.5 Angler profession bonus) (x1.4 Artisan profession bonus) = x4.2 of the base fish price
