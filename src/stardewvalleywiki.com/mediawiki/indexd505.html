The Slammer is a club weapon that can be obtained by breaking boxes and barrels, or as a special monster drop, on floors 40+ in the Skull Cavern. It is a possible reward for the chest on floor 110 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.

History

  • 1.5: Level raised from 5 to 7 and sell price raised from data-sort-value="250">250g to data-sort-value="350">350g.
