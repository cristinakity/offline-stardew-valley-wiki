Clock Ticking and Bouquet

Just a quick note to answer to "Bouquets are done smelting at the next tick of the in-game clock, which may be less than 10m" from Revision as of 16:52, 19 November 2021 by Margotbean. I would like to point out that is the case for every other durations (correct me if I'm wrong but on the game code, and in-game, it looks the same), e.g. 30m smelting for copper ores is three "ticks of the in-game clock", so technically the time is also ≤ 30m (and same for every other ones). I get that it may feel more clear to specify that for a 10m duration though. -- Charly (talk) 18:44, 19 November 2021 (UTC)
