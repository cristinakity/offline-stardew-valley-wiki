Parrot Egg is a trinket item dropped by slain monsters.

Equipping the parrot egg in the trinket slot causes a parrot to spawn and follow the player around. While in combat it occasionally finds gold coins when slaying monsters. The egg can be reforged using an Anvil, changing the level of parrot from 1 to 4, with a limit depending on your total earnings. Each possible level is equally likely.[1]

The chance of an enemy dropping a gold coin is 10% per level, for a maximum chance of 40% with a level 4 parrot, regardless of Luck or enemy type or location[2] Each gold coin is worth data-sort-value="250">250g, and for each gold coin an enemy drops, it has another chance to drop a gold coin.
