Dupe items in drop table?

What is the purpose of the doubled items in the drop table? I assume one of them is the chance for one to be dropped, and the other is a chance for a second to be dropped. If so, may be nice to have details on that explained on pages like this. If that's not it, then what're they there for? User:WhatAboutDustbunny (talk) 01:45, 13 March 2023

It doesn't really explain what the numbers are for, though, so potentially something like   Void Essence (99% first, 15% second)? The second might not fit well in the space, though.
