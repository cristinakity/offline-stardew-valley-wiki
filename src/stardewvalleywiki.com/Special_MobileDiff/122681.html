'''Fertilizer''' is used to support [[crop]] and [[Trees|tree]] growth and assist in [[farming]]. There are three types of crop fertilizer: Fertilizer, Retaining Soil and Speed-Grow. And there is one type of tree fertilizer: [[Tree Fertilizer]]. Some (crop) fertilizers are used by placing it on tilled soil before the planted seed sprouts; other types (of crop and tree) fertilizers can be used at any time during plant growth.  All crop fertilizers have three tiers, but all crop fertilizers affects all crops, including crops grown from [[Crafting#Seeds|Wild Seeds]].  Tree Fertilizer has one tier.
