Garlic is a vegetable crop that grows from Garlic Seeds after 4 days. The seeds are available at Pierre's General Store starting in Year 2.

Tailoring

Garlic can be used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the dyeable Shirt And Belt.

Quests

  • A fully-grown and unharvested Garlic is required for Gourmand Frog's puzzle on Ginger Island.

History

  • 1.4: Can now be used in Tailoring.
  • 1.5: Iridium quality is now possible when using Deluxe Fertilizer.
