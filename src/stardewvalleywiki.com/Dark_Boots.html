Dark Boots are a footwear item in Stardew Valley. They can be purchased from the Adventurer's Guild after reaching floor 80 in The Mines, or found in Fishing Treasure Chests. They may also be obtained by breaking boxes and barrels, or as a special monster drop, in the Skull Cavern (Floor 40+). They are a possible reward for the chest on floor 80 of the Mines if "remixed" mine rewards are selected in the Advanced Options menu when starting a new game.
