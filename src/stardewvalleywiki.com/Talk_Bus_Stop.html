Black line in bus door

Before the 1.3 update, When the cutscene was seen of the Junimos or Joja workers repairing the bus, the door was closed with a black line in it instead of the door not being there. Also, I've seen videos on YouTube where they were playing before the 1.3 update released. And they saw it. 9000 (talk) 8:43, 18 July 2022 (UTC)

v1.01 has no such door. If you can find the v1.11 or v1.2 data files, we can add the sprite to the page as a trivia item. margotbean (talk) 14:39, 18 July 2022 (UTC)
