Way Of The Wind pt. 2
You run a little bit faster.
Information
Source Bookseller

Way Of The Wind pt. 2 is a book that can be obtained by purchasing from the Bookseller for data-sort-value="35000">35,000g provided the player has read Way Of The Wind pt. 1 before.

Reading this book for the first time grants a unique power which provides a permanent +0.25 Speed bonus. Reading additional copies gives the player 20 XP in all skills.

Once read, it can be found in the player's menu on the Special Items & Powers tab.

The speed increase from this book stacks with Way Of The Wind pt. 1 (which is needed to acquire it), totaling to a permanent speed bonus of +0.5.
