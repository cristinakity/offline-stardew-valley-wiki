Friendship Perfection

In the Locations section, the article lists the requirement for the stardrop resulting from friendship with player's spouse to be 12.5 hearts (which is 3125 points). The Stardew Checkup helper app, however, lists the requirement explicitly as 13.5 hearts (3375 points). I don't know which is correct, but I thought maybe it might be useful to check. MouseyPounds has always been very reliable, and it would be good for both to be in synch. Thanks! Giles (talk) 03:48, 18 October 2023 (UTC)
