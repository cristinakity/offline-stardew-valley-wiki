The Piña Colada is a drink that can be purchased from Gus' bar for data-sort-value="600">600g when he visits the Beach Resort. It cannot be cooked, crafted, or obtained in any other way.

Tailoring

Piña Colada is used in the spool of a Sewing Machine to create the Black Leather Jacket.  

Notes

  • Though it cannot be cooked, its underlying category in the game's data files is "Cooking", so Piña Colada shows up on the "Cooked Items" tab of the Gift Log on the Social tab in-game after giving it as a gift.
  • Because it is a "cooked" item, it cannot be added to the Luau soup.

Trivia

  • A Piña Colada is typically an alcoholic cocktail, and the item shares the description "Drink in moderation" with the other alcoholic beverages in the game. However, drinking Piña Colada does not apply the Tipsy debuff.
  • Penny can recommend Piña Colada as a gift for her mother.
