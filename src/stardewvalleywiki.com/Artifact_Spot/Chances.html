This page shows the calculated chance to find an item from an artifact spot during the main spawning cycle.

This does not include Mystery Boxes, "rare items" (Golden Animal Crackers, a cosmetic item, or a skill book); nor does it include the Calico Eggs during the desert festival, nor the Golden Coconut, Taro Tubers, or Artifact Troves from the first spawning attempt on Ginger Island.

Note that the chances listed below add up to more than 100% as the Qi Beans (only available during the Quest) and the Bone Fragments (only available during or after completing Gunther's Special Order), indicated with a "*", will not stop the process resulting in an additional item spawning. With these removed, any row which does not add to 100% is due to rounding errors as all percentages are shown to 2 decimal places. A "-" indicates that this item cannot spawn here.
