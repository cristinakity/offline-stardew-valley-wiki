Pet Bowl
Provides a dedicated home for one pet.
Information
Build cost: data-sort-value="5000">5,000g
Build materials: Hardwood (25)
Size: 2x2

The Pet Bowl is a farm building purchasable from Robin at the Carpenter's Shop. There are 3 variants, which change slightly depending on the season. One free Pet Bowl is provided to the player at the start of their file for their free pet. This free Pet Bowl remains on the farm, even if the player declines to adopt the pet.

Additional Pet Bowls do not have to be constructed before adopting more pets from Marnie's Ranch. However, for every night that a pet does not have its own Pet Bowl, they will lose 10 friendship points the next morning.[1]
