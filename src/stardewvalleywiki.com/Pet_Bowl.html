Pet Bowl
Provides a dedicated home for one pet.
Information
Build cost: data-sort-value="5000">5,000g
Build materials: Hardwood (25)
Size: 2x2

The Pet Bowl is a type of farm building purchasable and upgradeable from Robin at the Carpenter's Shop. There are 3 variants, which change slightly depending on the season. One free pet bowl is provided to the player at the start of their file for their free pet. However, the free pet bowl remains on the farm even if the player declines to adopt the pet.

It is recommended to have one pet bowl per pet otherwise they will not be happy.

  • 1.0: Introduced.
