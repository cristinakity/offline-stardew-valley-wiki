This category contains subcategories and pages related to images of flooring that can be purchased from Pierre's General Store or through the Catalogue.

It does not include images related to crafted flooring or paths. For crafted flooring or paths the category is Category:Decor. To add an article, image, or category to this category, append [[Category:Flooring images]] to the end of the page.
