Profit Calculation

Trivia section net profit calculation was added unsourced and seemingly never discussed or verified. I'll walk through a quick profit calculation here so we can more easily verify the values in future.

Since resources sometimes give more than 1 item we need to account for the average number given: (0.3 × 1) + (0.3 × 3) + (0.3 × 5) + (0.09 × 10) + (0.01 × 20) = 3.8 items per resource

And since stone only uses this value half the time we need to account for that too: (0.5 × 3.8) + (0.5 × 1) = 2.4 stone per stone
