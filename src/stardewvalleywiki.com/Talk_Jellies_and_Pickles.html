Separate Pages for Jelly and Pickles

Why are these the same page? Pickles and Jelly are definitively different items, they have different IDs, generic Pickles and Jelly sold by the travelling cart have different values, they're tailored into different items, they have different gifting tables... they should be separate pages IMO.
