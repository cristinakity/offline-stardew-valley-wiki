Mystic Syrup is a Tree sap that can be obtained by tapping a Mystic Tree growing on the Farm and waiting 7-8 days.

Quests

Mystic Syrup is not used in any quests.

Tailoring

Mystic Syrup is not used in any tailoring. Mystic Syrup can be used in dyeing, serving as a blue dye.

Raccoon's Wife's Shop

Once unlocked, the Raccoon's wife near Giant Stump will trade with Mystic Syrup.
