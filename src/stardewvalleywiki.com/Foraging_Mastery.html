Foraging Mastery can be obtained after the player gains access to the Mastery Cave and completes a level of mastery by obtaining 10,000 Mastery points. It is the second totem from the left and is represented by an Axe. By unlocking this Mastery the player gains the Mystic Tree Seed and Treasure Totem recipes, and the Foraging Mastery power, which allows the player to find Golden Mystery Boxes.

Name Rewards Power
‎
Foraging Mastery
Mystic Tree Seed recipe This can be planted to grow a special tree. You can now find Golden Mystery Boxes, which contain superior items.
Treasure Totem recipe Use on diggable terrain to summon a ring of treasure spots.
