Rarecrow #7

Where to get Rarecrow #7? This article says it will be granted on 20 Museum donations, though I have ~35 now (last present was the bag of pumpkin seeds) and the Museum page also says that one will receive a painting decoration on 20 donations (and I actually have that deco item, so it seems to be true). --NobbZ (talk) 12:38, 23 January 2022 (UTC)

I have found a thread in the official forums that seems to answer my question. The Rarecrow #7 is only awarded after donating **artifacts**, not counting minerals. --NobbZ (talk) 12:40, 23 January 2022 (UTC)
Glad you figured it out! margotbean (talk) 15:30, 23 January 2022 (UTC)
