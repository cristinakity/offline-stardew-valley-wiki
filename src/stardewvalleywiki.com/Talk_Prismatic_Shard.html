Reaching Locations

Second from the last in the Locations section, the "top of the Volcano Dungeon" is mentioned as the place with a chest that contains a Prismatic Shard. What does "top" mean here? Wouldn't that be the entrance? One can only assume that you descend into the depths of a dungeon. Giles (talk) 03:57, 16 July 2022 (UTC)

Unlike The Mines and the Skull Cavern that both go downwards, the Volcano Dungeon is a mountain, so it goes upwards. So the "top" is level 10 and the entry is level 0. IBugOne (talk) 06:44, 16 July 2022 (UTC)

Renews?

I'm pretty sure the prismatic shard on the volcano dungeon renews, I've gotten one from it 3 times each time 1 week apart(edit from me, someone maybe me needs to check this) --Coffeefish8344 (talk) 19:17, 22 July 2022 (UTC)

Pretty sure it doesn't. That one is obtained from a chest in front of the Forge. The chest doesn't respawn and neither does the prismatic shard. --IBugOne (talk) 21:14, 22 July 2022 (UTC)
