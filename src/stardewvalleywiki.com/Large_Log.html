Large Log
Information
Source The Farm • Entrance to Secret Woods • The Mines (Shrine ON)
Season  All

Large Logs are fallen trees found on the Farm. A single large log is found in Cindersap Forest (blocking the entrance to the Secret Woods). Another large log is found somewhere on the right between the Blacksmith and the JojaMart. Large Logs can also be found in the Mines (floors 41-69 with Shrine of Challenge activated). A Steel Axe (or better quality) is required to remove a large log, yielding 8 Hardwood (10 with the Forester profession) and 25 Foraging Experience points. Large Logs will never reappear once removed.

Entrance to the Secret Woods location with the log blocking it.

Trivia

Destroying a large log takes:

  • 9 hits with a Steel Axe
  • 7 hits with a Gold Axe
  • 6 hits with an Iridium Axe
  • 5 hits with a Powerful Gold Axe
  • 3 hits with a Powerful Iridium Axe
