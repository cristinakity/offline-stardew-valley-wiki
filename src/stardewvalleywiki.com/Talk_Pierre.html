Dislike

Hi! Not sure how to edit dislikes on here but I have found out that Pierre dislikes Dinosaur Eggs! Mimiishappy (talk) 21:22, 23 May 2022 (UTC)

Dinosaur Eggs are considered Artifacts for purposes of gifting, not Eggs. Artifacts as a group are considered a Universal Dislike--all villagers dislike them with only a couple of exceptions. You will note that under Pierre's Dislikes, it says he dislikes all the Universal Dislikes except Fish (you can click through to see all the Universal Dislikes if you're not sure). Since Artifacts are not one of Pierre's exceptions to the Universal Dislikes, it means he dislike Artifacts, including Dinosaur Eggs. Hope that helps. Zendowolf (talk) 05:27, 24 May 2022 (UTC)

Ohhhh ok got it. Thank you so much!!! Mimiishappy (talk) 00:28, 25 May 2022 (UTC)

Unmentioned quote?

So the other day i was playing, during the fall. dunno if it's documented anywhere on the wiki, but, yeah, every character can tell you about the gift tastes of their friends and family. Atravita (talk) 16:14, 23 October 2022 (UTC)
It's mentioned in the first paragraph of Friendship#Gifts, that family and friends can give hints about what gifts an NPC likes. There is some debate about the "Friends" field in the infobox at the top right of every villager's page, some think it should list only people who will give gifting hints, but currently it lists more friends than that.
Anyway, yes, if you click on Pierre to talk to him, he may randomly say "Abigail loves (item), you could really make her happy with that" or other variations.
