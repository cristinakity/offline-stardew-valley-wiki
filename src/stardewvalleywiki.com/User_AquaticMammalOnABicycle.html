Dwarf Scroll IV

Hello! I saw the comment you made about your tip being removed from the Dwarf Scroll IV page, and wanted to give you an explanation. I'm one of the Admins of the wiki, so if you ever have questions, leave a note on my talk page.

The part about luck playing a role in finding the Scroll was false, that's true, but I removed the tip because my impression from reading reddit, discord, and the forums was that any of the Dwarf Scrolls could be difficult to obtain, and Scroll IV wasn't any more difficult than any other. So, killing monsters and tilling soil in the mines are both good options if the player is having difficulty finding them. Staircases can also be used to reach the floors of the mines with the most tillable soil (e.g., floor 93). So the tip seemed out of place given that context -- why point out one method without the others?

However, I haven't been a new player in quite some time, so I realize that your tip may be useful to newer players. I've shortened it, and added Scroll I to the tip, since the method of farming a floor repeatedly applies to it as well.

Hope this helps clear things up! Thank you for trying to make the wiki more helpful! margotbean (talk) 18:06, 5 October 2022 (UTC)
