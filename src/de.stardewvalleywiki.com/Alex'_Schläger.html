Alex's Bat is a club weapon sold by Alex for data-sort-value="70"> 70 Calico Eggs during the Desert Festival. Note that the Grand Slam featured in the description is not featured anywhere else in the game.

History

  • 1.1: Introduced but unobtainable.
  • 1.6: Now attainable during Desert Festival.
